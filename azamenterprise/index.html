Our Clients

We Provide the workers to different Multinational Companies to their Projects

Azam Enterprise was established in 1977. Once it was established, we got the opportunities to provide the workers to different Multinational Companies to their Projects in home and abroad.In addition, we are providing workers to our clients - through our Associates from India and Nepal. So far, we have employed around 31,000 workers in domestic projects and recruited around 14,000 workers for overseas projects – up to 2014. In such way, Azam Enterprise has gathered a long experience in Workers Recruitment and Management Services by providing professionals, skilled, semiskilled and general workers. We have also earned a considerable experience for handling local workers, Trade Unions, labor disputes and continue to specialize in this area. Moreover, we provide our Services to our clients for procurement of construction materials and leasing of vehicles in Bangladesh.
